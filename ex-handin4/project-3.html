Objects as social agents


Under the larger umbrella of design as a social and political force, this research project sets out explore objects (artefacts) as social agents for interconnection. Through a series of workshops I will investigate objects as mediator of empathic one-on-one encounters — akin to object-based social experiments of small-scale.
